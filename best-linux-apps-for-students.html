Best Linux Apps for Students

Practical, readable guidance for people who want useful tech without noise.

LinuxStudents

Linux has more useful software than people expect. If you only think of terminals and advanced tools, you are missing a lot.

LibreOffice

Still one of the most practical office suites for students.

Obsidian

Great for notes, linked thinking, and long-term organization.

VS Code

A strong choice for coding, markdown, and even structured study notes.

GIMP

Not perfect, but still useful when you need image editing without paying.

LocalSend

One of the easiest ways to move files between devices on the same network.

Final thoughts

The best Linux apps are the ones that remove friction from daily work, not the ones that only look cool in screenshots.
