Comandos copiables para instalar el setup en otra laptop con Zorin OS

Opción con wget:

wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Bocchi1204/clarity-desk/main/tools/setup-zorin-f-completo.sh
bash setup-zorin-f-completo.sh

Opción con curl:

curl -LO https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Bocchi1204/clarity-desk/main/tools/setup-zorin-f-completo.sh
bash setup-zorin-f-completo.sh

Si quieres hacerlo ejecutable primero:

wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Bocchi1204/clarity-desk/main/tools/setup-zorin-f-completo.sh
chmod +x setup-zorin-f-completo.sh
./setup-zorin-f-completo.sh
